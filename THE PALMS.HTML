WELCOME TO THE PALMS HOTEL

Located along the golden sandy beaches of Beruwela, Palms Hotel is your ideal place for a beach holiday. Surrounded by natural and historic wonders and a range of water based activities to keep you well geared the hotel has well appointed rooms facing the Indian Ocean. Guests can also enjoy delicious meals from Buffets, to a la carte and seafood treats. You can spend your holiday by enjoying an early morning swim at the hotel's outdoor pool or enjoy snacks from the coffee shop during lazy afternoons.





CONTENTS

Come to our hotel to enjoy the beauty of the beach with the beautiful environmental experience in Beruwala.

RESTAURANT

The hotel's main open-air restaurant serves buffet-style breakfast,lunch and diner as well as a la carte dining, and overlooks the pleasing sights and sound of its lush gardens. And for seafood lovers, the 'Fisherman's Wharf', our outdoor specialty seafood restaurant,serves up a sumptuous seafood treat.

BEACH AND WATER SPORTS

Beruwala,home to The plams, is one of Sri Lanka's best locations for water sport including wind surfing, parasailing,water skiing,water scooter rides,and river cruising. It also has facilities for deep-sea fishing, and wreck and reef diving.

BED ROOMS

Located in the heart of Aspen with a unique blend of contemporary luxury and historic heritage, deluxe accommodations, superb amenities, genuine hospitality and dedicated service for an elevated experience in the Rocky Mountains.
